How It Works

A clear path to your financial goals.

Every step is designed around you — your values, your family, and what you want your future to look like.

1
Step One Free

Strategy Meeting

A free, no-commitment conversation to find out if working together makes sense. We'll cover:

  • Go over your goals, values & priorities
  • Learn more about your situation
  • What value can I add for your family?
  • Would you benefit from working with me?
  • What makes me different from other financial professionals?

No commitment. No pressure. No cost. After the meeting, we'll decide together if it makes sense to continue.

2
Step Two

Plan Creation

We take everything we've learned and build a personalized financial plan designed around your life. This is where your goals, investments, taxes, and risk management all come together into one clear strategy.

  • Analyze your current financial picture (income, assets, liabilities, and goals)
  • Identify gaps, risks, and opportunities
  • Develop tax-efficient strategies tailored to your situation
  • Align your investments with your goals and timeline
  • Evaluate insurance and risk management needs
  • Map out your retirement income strategy
  • Coordinate estate and legacy planning considerations
  • Create a clear, actionable plan you can confidently follow

During this meeting, we'll walk through the completed plan together so you fully understand how everything works and why it is recommended.

3
Step Three

Plan Delivery

This is where we bring your plan to life. We'll walk through your strategy together so you understand exactly how everything fits — and what steps to take next.

  • Review your financial plan in a clear, easy-to-understand way
  • Explain the "why" behind each recommendation
  • Answer any questions and make adjustments as needed
  • Prioritize action steps so you know what to do first
  • Identify what we will help implement vs. what you'll handle
  • Align on timelines and next steps

You'll leave this meeting with a clear path forward.

4
Step Four ⭐ Most Important

Ongoing Support

We formally meet twice a year to ensure everything gets implemented and adjust as life changes. However, you can reach out anytime in between.

Spring Meeting
Review progress, action item check-in, update your plan
Fall Meeting
Year-end tax planning, investment review & plan updates
Anytime
Call or email whenever something comes up. No need to wait.
